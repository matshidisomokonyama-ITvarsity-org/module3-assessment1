Summertime limeade fizzle

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 16

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Squeeze lime juice from limes into a pitcher.
  2. Add 5 of the lime halves into the lime juice.
  3. Stir club soda, sugar, and salt into lime juice until sugar dissolves.
  4. Fill the rest of the pitcher with water and stir again.
  5. Serve Chilled
